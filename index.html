General Help


    Solves: Gain these by messing around with the 0's and 1's.
    Qlavrams: Generated by Randforcers. Formula is below, but you'll probably just look at the QPT.
    Cracks: Generated by Base Cracks. Formula is below.
    Base Cracks: Generated by line's 6-10.
    Performance: How well the game is doing.
    QPT: The amount of Qlavrams you get per tick. QPS will be soon once we figure out how to do it.
    Randforcers: Randomly changes every digit to a 1 or 0, seems good and op but actually pretty wack.
    Bruteforcers: Tries every single combination possible.
    Multipliers: If you've played an idle game before you know what these do. They just multiply their specified currencies by themselves.
    Solver: Main page of the game, where it actually happens.
    Upgrades: Buy upgrades here that make the game faster.
    Prestige: Reset everything for a powerful currency & boost.
    Achievements: Small goals that give rewards and a small boost, optional but makes the game faster so I encourage you to do these.
    Contacts: maybe? soon? idk
